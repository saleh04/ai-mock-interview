# Constraints

- The system must rely on free or open-source tools
- Real-time processing must work within limited hardware
- Some features may be simplified

---

# Assumptions

- Users provide readable CVs
- Microphone access is available
- Question bank is available

---

# Risks

## 1. Real-time Voice Complexity
Handling streaming audio and latency may be challenging.

## 2. Lip Sync Integration
Avatar synchronization may require optimization.

## 3. Multi-Agent Coordination
Ensuring proper communication between agents.

## 4. Free Model Limitations
Accuracy and availability may vary.

---

# Mitigation Strategies

- Start with simplified pipelines
- Use lightweight models
- Modular architecture
- Incremental testing
